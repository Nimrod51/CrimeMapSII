Crime Heat Map

Created by: Alicja Balfanz, Nimrod Gavish, Diego Morales, Jeison Londoño, Cristhian Murcia

This map allows to see a crime heat map in the City of London based on data from the UK's police data website.

The map aggregates locations of Street Level crimes from the latest month, and only includes crimes that have an associated location (latitude, longitude). We are using our own WMS (GeoServer) to provide the London boroughs borders. Additionally, the map displays a WMS layer that includes the underground stations and a London borough layer, retrieved from UK's government data portal.

Please use the layer control to change basemaps and overlay the heat and underground stations layers.
